Why Shroud?

🕸

No servers

Everything travels peer-to-peer through Tor hidden services. There's no backend to breach, subpoena, or shut down.

🔐

End-to-end encrypted

Ed25519 signatures, XChaCha20-Poly1305 AEAD, Argon2id password hashing. Your keys live only on your device.

🎙

Voice over Tor

Calls use the same signed-envelope channel as text. No WebRTC, no STUN, no TURN — your IP never leaks.

🆔

No accounts

Your identity is a .onion address generated locally on first launch. Nothing to sign up for, nothing to verify.

🛡

Verified contacts

Your first signed message from each contact pins their public key. Anyone spoofing the address later is detected and blocked.

🌍

9 languages

English, Ukrainian, Spanish, German, French, Portuguese, Polish, Turkish, Chinese — and growing.

How it works

  1. 1

    Install & launch

    Tor is bundled with the installer. On first run, Shroud generates your keys and a .onion address locally.

  2. 2

    Share your address

    Send your .onion address to whoever you want to chat with — it's a 56-character string, unique to you.

  3. 3

    Talk

    Messages, files, voice calls — all signed, encrypted, and routed through Tor. No third party can see who you're talking to.
